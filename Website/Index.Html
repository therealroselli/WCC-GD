Call of Duty Modern Warfare 2 2022
Call of Duty has been a revolutionalizing game in the industry. They have made statements in the game industry on what a FPS should be like. But the story is different from every different type of story line. In this Modern Warfare, you are playing as taskforce ghost, formally known as Taskforce 141, and are trying to take down a dangerous terrorist group.The characters you play as in the story are your protagonist and anyone who you kill is an antagonist, but the main antagonist is Hassan Zyani. After nyou beat the campaign, its your decsion on what's next, play online multiplayer, in which your antagonist is anyone on the other team, or warzone, which its everyone against you and your squad. The choice is yours!
The gamemechanics of Call of Duty have pretty standard over the years since the first Call of Duty. The ability to jump and vault over objects by the use of a specific set keybind, xbox would be the A button. Next the ability to prone or crouch through the B button, and push in the left-stick to sprint. But diffrent to other games COD has the ability to manualy reload and switch your weapons through a specific keybind. Other set of gamemechanics involve the ability to customize your guns through the loadoout tab in the menu. The other aspect of the game is being able to select what gamemode you want to play, ranginng from old classic team deathmatch all the way to search & destroy, there are tons of different games you can play in COD.
The range of characters ranges as time goes on throughout the games time period. The selection of characters in the game are divided into two seperate teams, Kortac & SpecGru. SpecGru consisits of all team ghost members from the campaign such as, Captain Price, Sgt. Gaz, Ghost, Alejandro, Soap, and many other characters that fight alongside the squaddron. Kortac's characters are alll the characters who are trying to go against the SpecGru squad. Each character has their own set of voice lines and personalities, Ghost is more quiet and stealthy, as where Captain Price is a leader role for the squad. Each character you can find out about their tone, attitudes, and personalities through playing the campaign and listening to their voice lines.
